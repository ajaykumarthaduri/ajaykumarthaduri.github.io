About Adarsha high school

Adarsha high School has assumed a credible position in terms of Quality Education among the most progressing educational institutions of Telangana Region for more than 29 years. The exponential growth itself is an indication of sincere efforts of Founder and Chairman who follows the personal principle of promoting uncompromising value-based quality education which promotes a global outlook that is needed for this new generation students. Pleasant atmosphere and Modern facilities make the students to get experience the joy of learning.

The best academic support and personal care is provided to the students with special focus in building a strong foundation of knowledge and concepts for their success.
