Academics

Achieving academic excellence is the hallmark of our School. We are affiliated to the State Board of Secondary Education

Our schools are affiliated by the board of Secondary Education. The planning of the program is well designed by our so called experts and the implementation is under the supervision of head of the institution. The faculty is well experienced to handle the programs efficiently and effectively. So that it is easy to receive the students without any stress. Special concentration and care have been taken for the slow learners and newly joined students and basic classes will be conducted for the beginners to improve their subject skills.

Pre-Primary

Pre-Primary section provides playway education.We offer early childhood education to children between the ages of 3to5.Our qualified teachers will guide the child to school readiness.Our pre-primary school help in oral and pre-writing language skills,pattern writing and physical movement skills.

The curriculum for Nursery, L.KG and U.KG is a child-centered and activity-based approach. A wide spectrum of training is given to the children covering Speaking, reading and writing skills. They are taught through the play-way method and the process of learning is joyful.

Primary

Our primary education program is intended to meet the requirements of each individual student,ensuring that each unique student receives personalised attention and assistance.We recognize that each kid is unique ,with various talents and learning styles.Primary school is very important for the growth of any individual as they can able to develop the good habits and qualities that help them in becomming a better person in future.

Primary education forms the bedrock of development.In primary school the children learn foundational skills that prepare them for life, work and active citizenship. Quality education empowers children and young people, safeguards their health and well-being.

Middle School

In the Middle School years, self-reliance in learning becomes an important aim. Attention is given to IIT, Medical and Civils foundation in each subject by defining standards. Collaborated learning is a regular feature at this stage, as children learn to work together, as well as independently. Children learn to set goals and have long term objectives.Responsible and upright behaviour is an expression of the school culture

We help a child to acheive the life goals by motivating them to better utilize new opportunities rather than getting into their comfort zone and continue to where they are for too long.

Secondary School

The knowledge and skills gained during the Secondary School years equip students with a firm academic foundation to pursue a variety of educational options in the future.Secondary education prepares the students for a specialized and independent approach to learning.Adarsha school provides education which transforms the students life.Our diverse range of subjects and extra curricular activities allows students to explore their passions and intrests.Throughout the curriculum, students are presented with opportunities to research, solve problems and come to their own conclusions and make decisions.
